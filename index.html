My other current dream is to get placed in an IT or ECE company and to introduce people with a new tech world. Here are some events where I have worked.

Hack In The North                                        IIITA_Hacks                                       OpenCode

Hack In The North is the India's largest student held hackathon conducted by students of IIIT Allahabad which promotes not only pro-developers but also gives amateur students a chance in creating and innovating solutions to the world's most pertinent problems.

IIITA-Hacks is a 17 hours mini hackathon held in IIITA. I was an active participant in '17, and participant and volunteer in '16.
Opencode is an open source event based on GSoC. It is organized by the technical society, Geekhaven members of IIIT Allahabad. Opencode strives to encourage open source development in students and also to create awareness about GSoC and open source development. I was an active participant during the event (17th jan,17 - 17th fab, 17). It was my second semester and first time I was having great experience of git and github.
